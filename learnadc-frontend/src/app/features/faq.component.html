Frequently Asked Questions

What is LearnADC?

LearnADC is a platform designed to teach players how to excel at the ADC role in League of Legends through video-based lessons.

Is LearnADC free to use?

Yes! LearnADC is completely free for users to learn and improve their gameplay.

Who can benefit from LearnADC?

Whether you're a beginner learning the basics or an experienced player looking to refine your mechanics, LearnADC is for you!

Will there be new courses added?

Yes, new lessons and courses will be added over time as the platform grows!
